Intersections & Constellations. From Gay to Queer

Since summer 2021, the working group "Queer narratives, mapped" has been working with our platform.

The result is the map From Gay To Queer that tells the story of how queer cultures and alliances have developed from lesbian and gay niches, with a temporal focus on the 1990s and a spatial focus on Bremen.

From interviews and the collection of flyers, posters, photos and other printed matter, a map has emerged that tells of different, personal perspectives on the emergence of queer communities and coalitions.
